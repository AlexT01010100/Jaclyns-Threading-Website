Threading

Providing brow and facial hair removal using thread. This method is known for its precision in shaping the eyebrows and for being gentle on the skin.

Microblading

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup technique where pigment is manually implanted under the skin using a handheld tool composed of several tiny, sterile needles. It creates the appearance of natural eyebrow hair.

Lash Lifting

Lash lifting is a semi-permanent treatment that gives your natural lashes an illusion of perfectly curled and longer lashes. This treatment involves lifting the lashes from the root and then setting them in place with a special solution.

Brow Lamination

Brow lamination is a process of restructuring the brow hairs to keep them in a desired shape. It creates shiny, smooth brows and can help to create a fuller look by lifting the brow hair.
