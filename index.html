Who Can Benefit From Microblading

Anyone can benefit from microblading! Want to correct asymmetrical eyebrows? How about thin or fading brows? Bad inflammation? Well no problem, have beauty at your fingertips in less than 24 hours!

The Healing Process

The process is painless, quick, and simple! After your initial microblading session, your skin should heal in 25 to 30 days. A few weeks after the initial microblading appointment, your brows will be safe from water. From there, You can continue your normal skincare and makeup routines!
