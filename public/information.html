Eyebrow Threading

Threading is the process where a thin cotton thread is used to remove unwanted hair from the root. The thread is twisted and rolled along the skin, trapping hairs and pulling them out quickly and precisely.

  • Duration: 15-20 minutes
  • Frequency: Every 2-3 weeks
  • Benefits: Precise shaping, no chemicals, gentle on sensitive skin

Microblading

Microblading is the process where pigment is manually deposited into the upper layers of skin using a specialized handheld tool with ultra-fine needles. This creates natural-looking, hair-like strokes that fill in sparse brows or reshape the entire eyebrow.

  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Results: Lasts 1-2 years
  • Includes: Consultation and touch-up session after 4-6 weeks

Lash Lifting

Lash lifting is the process where your natural lashes are lifted from the base and set in an upward curl using specialized solutions. This creates the appearance of longer, fuller lashes without the need for extensions or daily curling.

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Results: Lasts 6-8 weeks
  • Benefits: Low maintenance, enhances natural lashes, no extensions needed

Brow Lamination

Brow lamination is the process where brow hairs are chemically straightened and set in place to create a fuller, more uniform appearance. The treatment relaxes the hair follicle, allowing brows to be brushed upward and set in the desired shape.

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Results: Lasts 6-8 weeks
  • Benefits: Fuller appearance, tames unruly hairs, can be combined with threading

Microneedling

Microneedling is the process where fine needles create controlled micro-punctures in the skin. This triggers the body's natural healing response, stimulating collagen and elastin production to improve skin texture, reduce scarring, and promote overall rejuvenation.

  • Duration: 60-90 minutes
  • Treatment: 3-6 sessions recommended
  • Results: Visible improvement in skin texture and appearance after 4-6 weeks

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Eyebrow Threading?
A: Threading is a hair removal technique that uses twisted thread to pull hair from the follicle, commonly used for shaping eyebrows.

Q: Does threading hurt?
A: Some people experience mild discomfort, but it's usually less painful than other hair removal methods.

Q: What is Microneedling?
A: Microneedling is a procedure that uses fine needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin, stimulating collagen and elastin production.

Q: How long does it take to see results?
A: Results typically become visible after 4-6 weeks as collagen production increases. Multiple sessions may be recommended for optimal results.

Q: Is there any downtime?
A: Most people experience mild redness for 24-48 hours. The skin may feel slightly sensitive for a few days.

Q: What is Bioneedling?
A: Bioneedling is a natural variation of microneedling that uses organic, bio-compatible serums instead of synthetic ingredients, promoting natural skin healing and rejuvenation.

Q: How is it different from regular microneedling?
A: Bioneedling focuses on using all-natural, organic serums and botanicals, making it ideal for those who prefer holistic skincare treatments.

Q: Is it suitable for sensitive skin?
A: Yes, bioneedling's use of natural ingredients makes it particularly suitable for sensitive skin types.

Q: What is Brow Lamination?
A: Brow lamination is a process of restructuring the brow hairs to keep them in the desired shape, resulting in shiny, smooth brows.

Q: How long does the treatment last?
A: The effects of brow lamination can last up to 6-8 weeks.

Q: What is Lash Lifting?
A: Lash lifting is a treatment that gives your natural lashes an illusion of perfectly curled and longer lashes.

Q: Is it safe for my eyes?
A: Yes, lash lifting is safe when performed by a trained technician using high-quality products.
