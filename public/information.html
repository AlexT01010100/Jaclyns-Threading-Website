Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Eyebrow Threading?
A: Threading is a hair removal technique that uses twisted thread to pull hair from the follicle, commonly used for shaping eyebrows.

Q: Does threading hurt?
A: Some people experience mild discomfort, but it's usually less painful than other hair removal methods.

Q: What is Microneedling?
A: Microneedling is a procedure that uses fine needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin, stimulating collagen and elastin production.

Q: How long does it take to see results?
A: Results typically become visible after 4-6 weeks as collagen production increases. Multiple sessions may be recommended for optimal results.

Q: Is there any downtime?
A: Most people experience mild redness for 24-48 hours. The skin may feel slightly sensitive for a few days.

Q: What is Bioneedling?
A: Bioneedling is a natural variation of microneedling that uses organic, bio-compatible serums instead of synthetic ingredients, promoting natural skin healing and rejuvenation.

Q: How is it different from regular microneedling?
A: Bioneedling focuses on using all-natural, organic serums and botanicals, making it ideal for those who prefer holistic skincare treatments.

Q: Is it suitable for sensitive skin?
A: Yes, bioneedling's use of natural ingredients makes it particularly suitable for sensitive skin types.

Q: What is Brow Lamination?
A: Brow lamination is a process of restructuring the brow hairs to keep them in the desired shape, resulting in shiny, smooth brows.

Q: How long does the treatment last?
A: The effects of brow lamination can last up to 6-8 weeks.

Q: What is Lash Lifting?
A: Lash lifting is a treatment that gives your natural lashes an illusion of perfectly curled and longer lashes.

Q: Is it safe for my eyes?
A: Yes, lash lifting is safe when performed by a trained technician using high-quality products.
