Why Choose Jaclyn?

Information

Who Can Benefit From Microblading

Anyone can benefit from microblading! Want to correct asymmetrical eyebrows? How about thin or fading brows? Bad inflammation? Well no problem, have beauty at your fingertips in less than 24 hours!

Information

The Healing Process

Experience the ease of microblading with our painless and quick procedure. After your initial session, expect your skin to heal within 25 to 30 days. By the second week, your brows will safely repel water, allowing you to resume your skincare and makeup routines. Enjoy the beauty of natural-looking brows that require minimal maintenance!
