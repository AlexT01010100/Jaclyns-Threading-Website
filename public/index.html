About Jaclyn

Why Choose Jaclyn?

Jaclyn is a NYS licensed esthetician who has been expertly trained in the threading business and has been practicing for over 15 years. Jaclyn truly cares about her customers and consistently exceeds their expectations. She specializes in microblading and believes that everyone should live up their highest degree of beauty.
