About Me

Hi, I'm Jaclyn! Welcome to my salon, where beauty meets precision and every client is treated like family. I'm a New York State licensed esthetician with over 15 years of experience in the beauty industry, and my passion is helping people feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

My journey in the beauty industry began over 15 years ago, and I've dedicated myself to perfecting my craft ever since. I believe that beauty is about enhancing what makes you uniquely you, not changing it. Every treatment I perform is tailored to your individual needs, goals, and features.

What sets me apart is my attention to detail and genuine care for each person who walks through my door. I take the time to listen to your concerns, understand your goals, and explain every step of the process. My salon is designed to be a comfortable, welcoming space where you can relax and trust that you're in experienced, caring hands.

I'm continuously investing in my education, staying current with the latest techniques and trends in the beauty industry. My certifications and training allow me to offer a wide range of services, from traditional threading to advanced skin rejuvenation treatments. Quality and safety are my top priorities, which is why I only use professional-grade products and maintain the highest standards of hygiene.
