Welcome to Jaclyn's Threading

Welcome to Jaclyn's Threading, where beauty meets precision. We are dedicated to providing you with top-quality beauty services that enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence. With over 15 years of experience, Jaclyn is a New York State licensed esthetician who specializes in various beauty treatments. She is committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed your expectations.

At Jaclyn's Threading, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to your unique beauty needs:

Eyebrow Threading: Our threading service is renowned for its precision and gentleness, making it the perfect choice for shaping your eyebrows and removing unwanted facial hair. We use high-quality threads to ensure a smooth and comfortable experience, leaving your skin looking flawless.

Microblading: Our microblading service is ideal for those looking to achieve natural-looking, well-defined eyebrows. Using a handheld tool with tiny, sterile needles, we meticulously implant pigment under the skin to create the appearance of real eyebrow hair. This semi-permanent technique ensures long-lasting results that enhance your facial features.

Lash Lifting: For those seeking to elevate their lash game, our lash lifting service offers a semi-permanent solution that gives your natural lashes a beautiful, curled look. This treatment lifts your lashes from the root, creating the illusion of longer, fuller lashes without the need for extensions.

Brow Lamination: This treatment restructures your brow hairs, giving them a fuller and more defined look. The process involves lifting and setting the brow hairs in place, resulting in shiny, smooth brows that stay in shape for weeks.

Microneedling: Our microneedling service is a minimally invasive procedure that uses fine needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin. This stimulates collagen and elastin production, helping to improve skin texture, reduce scars, minimize fine lines, and promote overall skin rejuvenation.

Bioneedling: Combining the benefits of traditional microneedling with organic, bio-compatible serums, bioneedling offers a natural approach to skin rejuvenation. This treatment promotes collagen production while infusing the skin with nourishing botanical ingredients for enhanced healing, radiance, and a healthy glow.

Jaclyn truly cares about her customers and is passionate about helping them achieve their highest degree of beauty. Whether you are looking for precise threading, expertly done microblading, stunning lash lifts, perfectly laminated brows, or advanced skin rejuvenation treatments, Jaclyn's Threading has got you covered. Book an appointment today and experience the artistry and care that sets us apart.
