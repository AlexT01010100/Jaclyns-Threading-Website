Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Jaclyn's Beauty ("I," "me," or "my"). I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit my website or use my services.

2. Information I Collect

I collect information that you provide directly to me when you:

  • Book an appointment
  • Contact me through the contact form
  • Sign up for my newsletter or promotions
  • Interact with my website

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Appointment preferences and service history
  • Any other information you choose to provide

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect to:

  • Schedule and manage your appointments
  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Improve my services and website experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. I may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating my website and conducting my business (e.g., email service providers, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect my rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of assets, or acquisition

5. Data Security

I implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to the processing of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact me at JTSalon2018@gmail.com or (716) 400-1627.

7. Cookies and Tracking

My website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the website.

8. Third-Party Links

My website may contain links to third-party websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. I encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

My services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe I have collected information from a child under 13, please contact me immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
